About East African distributors Limited

East African Distributors Limited is a privately-owned company specializing in the large-scale importation and distribution of fast-moving consumer goods (FMCGs), particularly in the Foods & Beverages sector. We pride ourselves on building strong partnerships with global manufacturers while maintaining a robust local presence. Our company bridges the gap between producers and consumers by ensuring that essential products are delivered with speed, integrity, and care. With a growing footprint across the region, we are committed to driving economic growth, supporting job creation, and fostering innovation in distribution.


Our History
